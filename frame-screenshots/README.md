# Frame reference for Pixel Cluster Party

Screenshots are named by timestamp; sort by filename (earliest to latest) to view the animation in order.

**Intended motion (from asset descriptions):**
- **Layout:** Grid of small black squares (~6×8 or similar), or three vertical pixels that disperse.
- **Grid + burst:** At random times, a colored (orange/lavender) burst expands quickly from behind a black square then retracts. Motion is fast, stepped (pixel jump), asynchronous.
- **Easing:** Linear or stepped for bursts; no smooth interpolation to keep a low frame-rate / retro look.

Use these frames to verify and tune timing, step size, and burst behavior in `pixel-cluster-party.js`.
